At Aperture Laboratories Incorporated, we care about nature. That’s why we’ve started the Nature Observation and Transport Reservatory by Aperture Laboratories

N.O.T. ReAL

Our aim is to monitor and protect the sacret nature we were given.
Therefore we have ceased control over several large nature reservatories around the globe.
